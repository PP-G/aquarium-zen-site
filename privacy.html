Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Aquarium Zen: Retro is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not create user accounts, collect personal information, or track your activity. This policy explains how the app handles data.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Aquarium Zen: Retro operates entirely offline and does not:

  • Collect, store, or transmit any personal data
  • Require user accounts, registration, or sign-in
  • Use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs
  • Access your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other device sensors
  • Connect to the internet for any purpose

Local Data Storage

The app stores your preferences (such as fish count, background style, fish speed, screensaver timer, and focus timer settings) locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults system. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Is not backed up to any external server

Third-Party Services

Aquarium Zen: Retro does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, APIs, or analytics platforms. There are no third-party components in the app.

Children's Privacy

Because Aquarium Zen: Retro does not collect any data from any user, it is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction) because we do not collect personal information from anyone.

Your Rights Under GDPR (European Union)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Because Aquarium Zen: Retro does not collect, process, or store any personal data, these rights are inherently satisfied:

  • Right of Access: There is no personal data to access.
  • Right to Rectification: There is no personal data to correct.
  • Right to Erasure: There is no personal data to delete. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored preferences.
  • Right to Data Portability: There is no personal data to transfer.
  • Right to Object: There is no data processing to object to.

No data controller or data processor exists for this app, as no personal data is processed.

Your Rights Under PIPEDA (Canada)

If you are located in Canada, you have rights under the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA). Because Aquarium Zen: Retro does not collect, use, or disclose any personal information:

  • Consent: No consent is required as no personal information is collected.
  • Access: There is no personal information to access.
  • Accuracy: There is no personal information to correct.
  • Safeguarding: No personal information is stored, so no safeguards are necessary beyond your device's built-in security.
  • Openness: This policy constitutes our full disclosure of information practices.

Your Rights Under CCPA (California)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with specific rights. Because we do not collect personal information, sell personal information, or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising, all CCPA rights are inherently satisfied.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
